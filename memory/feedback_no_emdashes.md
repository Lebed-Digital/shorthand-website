Never use em dashes (—) in any written content — blog posts, comparison pages, landing pages, or UI copy.

**Why:** G's style preference. Use a comma, period, or rewrite the sentence instead.

**How to apply:** Before writing or committing any content, scan for — and replace or rewrite.
